About Knoocked Loose

We’re Knocked Loose — a hardcore band from Oldham County, Kentucky, built on raw emotion, chaos, and honesty. From the beginning, our goal’s been simple: to make music that hits hard and connects with people on a real, human level. Every show, every record, every scream — it’s a ll about release. We channel everything we’ve got into the sound: the anger, confusion, and hope tha t come from trying to make sense of the world around us.

Over the years, we’ve grown, but we’ve never lost that core i ntensity that defines who we are. Our music is fast, heavy, and unapologetically real — a mix of hardcore, metal, and the kind of vulnerability that only comes fr om living through it. We’re proud of the community that’s formed around what we d o, and we’ll keep pushing forward, louder and heavier, because that’s what Knocke d Loose is: pure energy, no filter, no compromise.
